**Drag tiles • Build words • Capture gold stars**

QWERTY is a colorful browser word game inspired by classic Pogo QWERTY. Place letters from your rack onto a linked board, form words in rows or columns, and race the computer to **1,000 points** while capturing **gold stars** for bonus scoring.

## Features

- Drag-and-drop tile placement on board and rack
- Play against the computer (Easy, Medium, or Hard)
- Linked boards — once connected, play on both players' words for link bonuses
- Gold star squares (+50 bonus when covered)
- Blank wild tiles — choose any letter
- Exchange unwanted tiles from your rack
- 2-minute turn timer with audio cues in the final seconds
- Shuffle rack, recall tiles, and reorder letters on your rack
- Built-in game rules and 2-letter word reference
- Progress saves automatically in your browser

## How to play

1. Click **Run game** (or open the page in your browser).
2. Choose difficulty and start a new game or continue a saved game.
3. Place your **first word on your green corner square** (bottom-left).
4. On later turns, connect new words to words already on the board.
5. When your word is ready, click **Submit**.
6. First player to **1,000 points** wins.

**Controls:** Mouse or touch — drag tiles between your rack and the board.

## Tips

- Tap a rack tile twice to swap it with another slot, or drag to reorder.
- Use **Recall All** to bring unplayed tiles back from the board.
- Check **Game rules** and **2-letter words** from the legend at the bottom of the screen.

## Technical notes

- HTML5 / JavaScript — runs in modern desktop and mobile browsers.
- No install required when played in the browser.
- Save data is stored locally in your browser (not synced across devices).

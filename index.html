How to play

Four rules to start

Learn these in a minute. The full guide is in the game.

  1. Start on green Your first word must cover the green YOU square. Opponents start on amber P2.
  2. Connect your words Each new play shares a letter with the board — classic crossword rules.
  3. Hit gold stars Land on a star for +50. First to 1000 points wins.
  4. Invite a friend Create a room code, share the link, and chat while you play.

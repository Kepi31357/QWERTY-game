Rules teaser

Simple to start. Sharp to master.

Everything you need for the first game — the full rules live inside Play.

  1. Start from your corner Your first word must cover the green YOU square. Opponents start on amber P2.
  2. Connect every play New words share a letter with what’s already on the board — crossword style.
  3. Chase gold stars Land on a star for a +50 bonus. Race to 1000 points to win the match.
  4. Invite a friend Create a room code, share the link, and play live with chat on the side.

About

Made for lively word nights

QWERTY is a colorful word game inspired by classic online tile battles — built for quick matches against the computer or longer sessions with someone you know.

Pick Easy, Medium, or Hard, keep a private room for friends, and leave the dictionary arguments to the game.

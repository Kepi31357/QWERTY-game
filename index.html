Rules

Four rules to start

Learn these in a minute. Full rules are inside the game.

  1. Start on green Your first word covers the green YOU square. Opponents start on amber P2.
  2. Connect every play New words share a letter with the board — crossword style.
  3. Chase gold stars Land on a star for +50. First to 1000 points wins.
  4. Invite a friend Create a room code, share the link, and chat while you play.
